Boss Rush V1.0

Start with a Volt, Excalibur, or Mag
and your choice of any of the MK-1 weapons for each slot.
No operator allowed. Ideally if you have Vazarin as a focus school, select that as well.
No Parazon mods, no gear
You may choose one companion to bring with you, but they can only start with fetch/vacuum and (primed) animal instinct.
If robotic, assault mode is allowed as well. Weapons and claws must be otherwise unmodded until you earn the mods for them
